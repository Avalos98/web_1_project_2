Typography book

My final for my Typography 2 class was to make a book showcasing the drop caps we have developed over the year, following a theme of our choosing.

I spent alot of time sketching out letterforms, then I chose one of each letter to refine and digitize. After all the letters have been digitized, I started working on my book by developing a layout for my drop caps.

The last step was printing the pages, and making the book, which was a fun and stressful process.
